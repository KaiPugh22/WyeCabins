Hay Glamping

Our luxury pop-up glamping village, just a short stroll from the Hay Festival gates. Spacious bell tents with real beds, crisp linen, continental breakfast and a communal firepit to end the evening. Running since 2014, it's the perfect base for festival-goers who want comfort without compromise.

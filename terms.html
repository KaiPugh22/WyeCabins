1. Booking and payment

All bookings are made through our online booking platform or by contacting us directly via email. A non-refundable deposit of 25% of the total stay cost is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due no later than 42 days before your arrival date.

If your booking is made within 42 days of arrival, full payment is required at the time of booking.

2. Cancellation policy

  • More than 21 days before arrival: Your booking may be transferred to alternative dates at no additional cost, subject to availability. The deposit is non-refundable.
  • Less than 21 days before arrival: No refund and no rescheduling. The full booking amount is non-refundable.

We strongly recommend that you take out holiday insurance to cover cancellation costs.

3. Check-in and check-out

  • Check-in: From 4:00 PM on the day of arrival
  • Check-out: By 10:00 AM on the day of departure

Detailed directions and access instructions will be sent to you by email prior to your arrival.

4. Number of guests

Each cabin has a maximum occupancy as stated on the listing. The number of guests must not exceed the maximum capacity. Any additional guests not declared at the time of booking may result in the booking being cancelled without refund.

5. Dogs

Well-behaved dogs are welcome at our countryside cabins. Dogs must be kept under control at all times and must not be left unattended in the cabin. Please clean up after your dog on site and in the surrounding area. Any damage caused by dogs will be charged to the guest.

6. Smoking

All cabins are strictly non-smoking. Smoking is not permitted inside any cabin or enclosed structure. A cleaning charge of £150 will apply if evidence of smoking is found inside a cabin.

Guests are expected to take reasonable care of the cabin and its contents. You are responsible for any damage caused during your stay beyond normal wear and tear. Please report any damage or maintenance issues to us promptly.

The cost of repair or replacement for any damage will be charged to the guest.

8. Noise and behaviour

Our cabins are in rural, peaceful settings. We ask that guests are respectful of the environment and any neighbours. Excessive noise, particularly after 10:00 PM, is not acceptable and may result in your stay being terminated without refund.

9. Fire safety

Fire pits and wood burners are provided for your enjoyment. Please follow the safety instructions provided in the cabin. Fire pits must only be used in the designated areas and must be positioned at least 3 metres from any structure. Do not leave fires unattended. Ensure all fires are fully extinguished before leaving the cabin or going to sleep.

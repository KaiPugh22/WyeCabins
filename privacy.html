Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

Wye Cabins operates hand crafted cabin accommodation near Hay-on-Wye, Powys. Our site address is Gwernyfed Old Estate Yard, Felindre, Brecon, Powys LD3 0SU. When this policy mentions "we", "us" or "our", it refers to Wye Cabins.

Contact: info@wyeglamping.co.uk | Phone: 07974 000421

What data we collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Make a booking or enquiry (name, email address, phone number, dates of stay)
  • Subscribe to our mailing list (email address)
  • Contact us via our website form, email or phone
  • Visit our website (see Cookies section below)

How we use your data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and manage your accommodation booking
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you booking confirmations, directions and check-in information
  • Send marketing emails if you have opted in to our mailing list (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve our website and services

Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to fulfil your booking or respond to your enquiry
  • Consent: Where you have opted in to receive our newsletter or marketing communications
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our services and website functionality

Third-party bookings

Bookings are processed through our booking platform. When you make a booking, your data is shared with our booking provider to complete the reservation. Their privacy policy applies to data they hold.

Data sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Our booking platform to process reservations
  • Email service providers to send marketing communications you have opted into
  • Google (Maps embed on our website, Analytics if enabled)

How long we keep your data

Booking data is retained for up to 3 years after your stay for accounting and legal purposes. Mailing list data is kept until you unsubscribe. Enquiry data is kept for up to 12 months.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law (UK GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@wyeglamping.co.uk.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g. session management)
  • Google Maps: The embedded map on our unit pages may set cookies from Google
  • Instagram embed: The embedded Instagram feed may set cookies from Meta/Instagram

We do not currently use analytics tracking cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and provide a cookie consent mechanism.

Children's privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Bookings must be made by an adult.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at info@wyeglamping.co.uk.

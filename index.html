The antidote to modern life

Wye Glamping is perfect for a romantic retreat or just something a bit different. We offer everything you need to relax in style, surrounded by nature. Nestled in a magical corner of the Brecon Beacons National Park, you'd struggle to find a better site. We've got mountain views, a babbling brook, incredible wild flowers and oodles of space to explore. By night, sit around the fire pit under incredible starry skies.

Whether you're after a romantic retreat or just something a bit different, join us for the ultimate stress free holiday.
